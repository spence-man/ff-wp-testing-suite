What made sense for your parents may not work today. This class is designed to help guide you through the retirement maze and teach you how to plan smarter in the 21st century.

This course is held all around the country at universities, libraries, and community centers, but is not affiliated with any of these locations.

You will learn

  • 4 investor headwinds that make it difficult to get ahead in today’s market.
  • How to help guard your portfolio from the next market crash.
  • How to build a customized retirement program with stable income streams.
  • 5 rules from the world’s greatest investors almost everyone can use.
This educational course was developed for adults ages 50-70 with at least $250,000 of investible assets. There is no cost or obligation, but seating is limited so reserve your spot today.
